How to Meditate


A simple step-by-step guide to meditating for beginners and experts alike
1. Take a seat and find place to sit that feels calm, comfortable and quiet to you if you prefer.
2. Notice your body and how your body feels.
3. Truly feel your breath.
4. Notice when your mind has wandered and consider your mind state at the moment.
5. Take note of your surroundings: the sounds, the sensations, the smells, etc...
6. Enjoy as needed



Breathing Techniques & Breath Work


How To use the 4-7-8 breath work technique:
1. Focus on your breath specifically
2. Empty your lungs completely of air.
3. Breathe in quietly through the nose for 4 seconds.
4. Hold your breath for about 7 seconds.
5. Exhale forcefully through your mouth
6. Repeat this cycle up to 5 or more times.



Website Guide


1. How to use the Audio Player:
1. Go to the “Relax” section of the website
2. Browse through the collection of sounds by clicking on the right or left navigation buttons
3. Select one of the sounds from the sound library
4. Click on the desired sound icon button
5. Then select the play button in the audio player controls below the sound library
6. To pause the sound click the pause button
7. If you can’t decided on one sound try out the shuffle feature, to shuffle randomly through various sounds click the shuffle button
8. If you want to keep a sound playing for as long as you’d like click the repeat sound button and the sound will play for an indefinite amount of time
9. To customize sound settings further such as, EQ, Volume threshold, Randomize volume, randomize EQ, and more website settings, click on the gear icon to display the settings page
